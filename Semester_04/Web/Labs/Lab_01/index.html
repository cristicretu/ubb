Company History

Porsche is a German automobile manufacturer that was founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche. The company is known for producing high-performance sports cars and SUVs.

The first Porsche, the 356, was designed by Ferdinand "Ferry" Porsche, son of Ferdinand Porsche. The company has since grown to become one of the most respected sports car manufacturers in the world, known for its engineering excellence and motorsport success.

Motorsport Heritage

Porsche has a rich history in motorsport, with numerous victories in prestigious races around the world.

  • 24 Hours of Le Mans - 19 overall victories
  • World Sportscar Championship - 15 titles
  • Formula One - Participation as both constructor and engine supplier
  • Paris-Dakar Rally - Multiple victories with the 959
  • World Endurance Championship - Multiple championships
